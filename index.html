Who We Are

About Healthy Community

Uniting science, health, and opportunity to build stronger, healthier communities

Our Vision

A healthy, economically empowered, and environmentally sustainable community where every individual has access to healthcare, clean surroundings, and opportunities for a better life.

Our Mission

To improve health, reduce poverty, and protect the environment through community-based healthcare, economic empowerment, environmental education, and sustainable development programs.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Five principles that guide everything we do

Community Empowerment

We believe lasting change happens when communities lead their own development and take ownership of solutions.

Equity & Inclusion

We are committed to reaching vulnerable and underserved populations without discrimination.

Prevention & Sustainability

We focus on long-term solutions that prevent disease, reduce poverty, and protect the environment.

Integrity & Accountability

We operate with honesty, transparency, and responsibility to the communities and partners we serve.

Collaboration & Partnership

We work with local leaders, institutions, NGOs and partners to create lasting and meaningful impact.

What we have accomplished and where we are headed next

Activities Completed

Completed

Free Health Screening for the Aged

Kpandai District

Comprehensive health screening program targeting elderly community members in the Kpandai District, providing free blood pressure, glucose, and general health checks.

Completed

Hepatitis B Health Education

Kpandai District

Health education initiative focused on Hepatitis B virus awareness, prevention, and treatment options for middle-aged community members.

Completed

Cervical Cancer Awareness

Tamale Technical University (Zoom)

Virtual health education session on cervical cancer awareness, screening importance, and prevention strategies for university students and staff.
