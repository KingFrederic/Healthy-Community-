Vision

Healthy lives and a sustainable planet for every community.

Mission

To provide free, accessible health screenings to underserved communities while empowering individuals with knowledge and tools for environmental sustainability.

What we do

We run mobile clinics and community talks. We focus on quick checks and clear steps that save lives and protect the environment.

Blood Pressure Blood Sugar Vision BMI Malaria Eco Education

Programs

Two tracks. One purpose.

Community Health

Free checks for blood pressure, blood sugar, vision, BMI, and malaria. Quick triage. Fast referrals.

  • On-site counseling
  • Referral letters
  • Follow-up calls
  • Health cards

Environmental Education

Practical tips for clean air, safe water, and low waste. Tree planting. Community cleanups.

  • Waste sorting
  • Safe cooking
  • Water safety
  • Tree care
